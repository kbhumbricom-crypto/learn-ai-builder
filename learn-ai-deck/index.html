The internet doesn't have a knowledge problem.
It has a pedagogy problem.

Why would someone actually use this?

There are 200+ million courses online. Most of them are mediocre. The great ones are locked behind $2,000 paywalls or taught by someone whose style just doesn't click with you.

The best curriculum in the world is useless if the teaching style doesn't match the learner.

Core Value Proposition

"Learn anything, taught the way your brain actually learns."

It's not about generating content. It's about re-teaching existing knowledge in a voice that resonates with you.

The Market Landscape

Traditional EdTech

Coursera / Udemy

High structure, but zero personalization. You get what everyone else gets.

Generic AI

ChatGPT / Notion AI

High personalization, but no pedagogical structure. It's a tool, not a teacher.

Learn.ai is the only platform offering high structure (full syllabi, tracking) with high personalization (any voice, any style).

Distribution

Go-To-Market Motion

  • SEO / Programmatic SEO: Public directory of 100,000+ auto-generated courses ("Learn Python in 10 Days" taught by a pirate). Highly shareable, indexing immediately on Google.
  • TikTok / Shorts: "Study with me" viral clips showcasing the AI voiceover feature on complex topics.
  • B2B Direct Sales: Targeting Corporate L&D teams with stagnant completion rates. "We make your compliance training sound like a podcast."
Market Timing

Why Now?

1. AI Memory is Unlimited

Previously, AI couldn't remember a full course syllabus. Today, it has enough "memory" to instantly read and generate entire 12-week curriculums.

2. Perfect Human Voices

Robotic voices ruin the learning experience. New AI voice technology sounds perfectly human, making it enjoyable to listen and learn for hours.

Phased Rollout

Phase 1: Audio First (1-2 weeks)

Add "Listen" button. OpenAI TTS API. ~$0.015 per 1K chars. Highest ROI move.

Phase 2: Slide Decks (1-2 weeks)

Interactive slideshows synced to audio narration. The "Khan Academy" feel.

Phase 3: Full Video (3-4 weeks)

Remotion to render MP4 videos with subtle animations.

Phase 4: Avatar Presenter (Future)
