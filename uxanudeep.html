The Questions Designers Ask Before They Join

Frequently asked.

"What is the difference between Core, Premium, and Elite?"
All three give you the same six-month Gym … same phases, tracks, projects, workshops, Discord, and weekly Q&As with me. Core is the full Gym, self-driven, on open seats. Premium adds my eyes on your work … personalised roadmap, portfolio reviews, written feedback on every project. Elite is the closest seat … everything in Premium plus 3 dedicated 1:1 calls, bi-weekly check-ins, and call support before every interview. Premium and Elite are both by interview only … Core is the only tier on open seats.
"I am an experienced designer. Will this work for me?"
Yes. Your stage just changes the roadmap. At 10 LPA, we work towards 20. At 20, towards 40. At 40, towards leadership. The blockers at your stage are usually visibility gaps, not skill gaps … and that's exactly what we fix.
"I do not have a UX background. What if I am a fresher?"
Engineers, MBAs, architects, dentists, animators, freshers … all part of past Gyms. Your background is a feature, not a hurdle. Freshers actually have an edge … nothing to unlearn.
"I've already done UX courses. How is this different?"
This is a mentorship, not a course. You get me personally for six months … every weekly Q&A, every workshop, every project review. No recorded sessions replacing live ones. No proxy mentors.
"I have a portfolio but recruiters aren't responding. Can this fix that?"
Yes … this is exactly what we fix. Recruiters hire designer identities, not portfolios. In six months, the same projects get repackaged through the lens of a real designer, and your visibility goes up at every stage of the recruiter's journey.
"Will UX Anudeep actually be there throughout?"
Every weekly Q&A. Every workshop. Every project review. Discord every day. Yes … I'm there.
"I am in a full-time job. Can I still join?"
Yes. Live calls happen on weekends. Drills, projects and materials run at your own pace through the week. You don't have to abandon your job … you just need consistency.
"Will I learn AI tools properly?"
Yes … by using them on real projects. Phase 02 builds fluency in AI design tools and AI-based interaction patterns. Phase 03 takes you into Agentic UX, Generative UI, and end-to-end AI product design. You become the driver, not dependent on any one tool.
"Is EMI available?"
Yes, EMI options are available across all three tiers.
"When does the cohort start?"
The start date is shared with selected students once admissions close. Seats are first-come, first-serve.
